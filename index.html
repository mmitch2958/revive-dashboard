Executive Summary

The Harrisburg roofing market is competitive but digitally fragmented. The top players — The Exterior Company, ROOFX, and Greenawalt — dominate through review volume, manufacturer certifications, and insurance claim partnerships. But 7 of 9 competitors run on outdated website builders with poor SEO, slow load times, and no conversion optimization.

Revive has a significant opportunity to leapfrog the competition with a modern, fast website paired with targeted Google Ads and local SEO. The market gap is clear: nobody owns "roof restoration" in Harrisburg, and transparent, repair-first messaging is virtually absent.

💡 The Short Version

A professional website + $2K/month in Google Ads = ~15 qualified leads/month. One roofing job ($8,500 avg) pays for the entire website build. The competition isn't fighting for digital — they're fighting door-to-door.
