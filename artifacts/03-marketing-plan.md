## 1. Market Analysis

### Harrisburg Roofing Market Overview
The Harrisburg metropolitan area (pop. ~590,000 in the MSA) presents a strong market for roofing services. Pennsylvania experiences significant weather variation — heavy snow loads, ice dams, spring storms, and summer thunderstorms — creating consistent demand for roof repair and restoration. The Harrisburg area has a mix of aging residential stock (many homes 30-80 years old) and growing commercial development, both driving roofing needs.

### Competition Analysis (9 Competers Identified)
| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Wagner Roofing | Established reputation, large crew | Generalist focus, slower to adopt digital |
| Capital Roofing | Competitive pricing | Limited online presence |
| A1 Roofing | 24/7 emergency service | Mixed review quality |
| Above All Roofing | Strong commercial portfolio | Residential underserved |
| Baker Roofing | Large service area | Higher price point |
| C&C Roofing | Good local reviews | Minimal content marketing |
| L. George Roofing | Family-owned trust | Limited service variety |
| Rapid Roof Repair | Fast response times | Brand recognition low |
| Keystone Roofing | Insurance work expertise | Poor social media presence |

### Opportunity: Roof Restoration Niche Is Wide Open
Most competitors focus on full replacement or basic repair. There is a significant gap in **roof restoration** — extending roof life through coating, sealant, and targeted repair rather than full replacement. This positions Revive Roof Repair as a cost-effective alternative that delivers 80% of the value at 30-50% of the cost. No competitor currently owns this positioning in the Harrisburg market.

### Target Audience

**Primary — Residential Homeowners**
- Age: 35-65
- Home value: $150K-$450K
- Home age: 15+ years
- Pain points: Cost of full replacement, fear of leaks, insurance complexity
- Decision drivers: Trust, reviews, clear pricing, speed

- Need reliable, repeatable service

**Tertiary — Commercial Building Owners**

## 4. Review Management

### System Overview
- **Automated review requests** via SMS and email after every completed job
- **Timing**: Request sent 24-48 hours after job completion (sweet spot for positive sentiment)
- **Platform priority**: Google Business Profile > Facebook > Nextdoor > Yelp

### Goal: 4.8+ Stars on Google with 50+ Reviews
- **Timeline**: Reach 50 reviews within 6 months of launch
- **Strategy**: Every customer interaction ends with a review request
- **Training**: Crew trained to mention reviews at job completion

### Review Request Flow
1. Job marked complete in CRM
2. 24-hour delay
3. SMS sent: "Hi [Name], thanks for choosing Revive Roof Repair! How was your experience? Leave us a quick review: [link]"
4. If no response after 3 days → follow-up email with same request
5. Positive reviews → Thank you message + referral incentive
6. Negative reviews → Immediate internal alert → respond within 4 hours

### Respond to All Reviews Within 24 Hours
- **Positive**: Personalized thank you, mention specific detail from job
- **Negative**: Acknowledge, apologize, take offline ("Please call us at [number] so we can make this right")
- **Template library**: 15+ response templates customized per situation
- **Monthly review report**: Track trends, identify operational issues
